Read Poker Room Rules
  • Profanity or abuse of other players or dealers will not be tolerated.
  • It is the player’s responsibility to protect their own hand.
  • Cards speak. A player does not have to recognize the value of their own hand in order to win the pot.
  • A player’s seat will be saved for 15 minutes if they leave the table. After that time, their chips will be picked up and saved for them by the floor person.
  • Players may not switch tables without permission from the boardperson.
  • No tobacco products of any kind allowed.
  • English is the only language to be spoken at the table.
  • Cell phones are prohibited while at table.
  • Verbal action in turn is binding.
  • No side bets are permitted.
  • No string bets or string raises allowed.
  • Table stakes only.
  • In all games requiring a posting of blind bet, any missed blind must post a blind in order to be dealt the next hand. Otherwise, they may wait to play when it is their turn to be the blind.
  • No credit is extended by the house.
  • Straddle bets allowed.
  • Only one player per hand.
  • No racks on table. Please put them under your chair.
  • ALL FLOORPERSONS/MANAGEMENT DECISIONS ARE FINAL.
