Watch & Wager on Horse Racing and Jai‑Alai

Place your bets on all the non-stop horse racing action and exciting Jai-Alai from the nation's leading tracks and frontons. Our state-of-the-art room features individual stations with personal TVs along with giant LED screens so you can follow all the action all day long.

Never miss a race or a game with food service available. Wagers can be placed at self-service machines or with our friendly tellers. Don't miss the annual racing events including the Kentucky Derby and Breeders Cup along with special sports contests and promotions. Your home for sports and wagering is the Bonita Springs Poker Room!
