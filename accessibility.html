Accessibility

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Bonita Springs Poker Room is firmly committed to compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act.

As part of this commitment, we are continually improving our website to make it as accessible as possible. If you have any questions about the accessibility of the website, or if you have suggestions about improving the usability or accessibility of the website, please contact us.
